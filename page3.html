Delicious Tuna Casserole

This tuna casserole has become a staple in my household. This recipe is not only delicious but also quite easy. The majority of the ingredients come from cans of come straight off the shelf. As a quick note while most of the items do not need to be a specific brand, the mushroom soup must be campbells cream of mushroom. This recipe comes together easily and makes for a great meal for the entire family.

Ingredients

Metric units

  • 3 cups dry bite sized pasta
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 1 Stalk celery
  • 1 can campbell's cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can peas and carrots
  • 1 can tuna
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tsp parsley (divided)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar (divided)
  • 1/3 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 tbsp butter (divided)
  • Salt and pepper as desired

Recipe

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 fahrenheit. Mix bread crumbs, 1 tsp parsley, 1 tbsp butter (melted), and 1/4 cup shredded cheddar in a small bowl.
  2. Chop green onion and celery. Melt remaining butter in frying pan and fry celery and green onion until celery is soft.
  3. Cook pasta according to packaging.
  4. In a large bowl combine pasta, green onion and celery, mushroom soup,peas and carrots, tuna, milk, and remaining parsley. Transfer to a baking dish.
  5. Top with the bread crumb mixture and bake for about 20 mins
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
